# Next Steps Catalog — Post-Report Follow-Up

Maps delivered report product + recipient state to the right next step.
Pick one. Do not offer a menu in the follow-up email.

---

## After reclassification_candidates

| Recipient state | Next step |
|---|---|
| No reply yet | Offer a short call to walk through the top flags before their next curation cycle. One sentence, frictionless ask. |
| Replied with interest | Ask which specific variants they want to dig into first and offer to pull the supporting papers for those. |
| Has additional genes pending (e.g., OTC) | Offer to queue the next gene's report. Reference the pending gene by name. |
| Replied with a question about methodology | Answer the question directly in the email. No call needed unless they ask. |

## After conflict_summary

| Recipient state | Next step |
|---|---|
| No reply yet | Offer to filter the conflict list to only those with published papers that likely resolve them — the ones worth acting on now. |
| Replied with interest | Ask which conflicts they want to prioritize and offer to pull the supporting evidence for those. |
| Has additional genes pending | Offer to queue the next gene. |

## After vus_triage

| Recipient state | Next step |
|---|---|
| No reply yet | Offer to walk through the top 5 — the ones closest to a classification given available literature. |
| Replied with interest | Ask which tier they want to focus on first and offer to surface the specific papers for those variants. |

## After per_paper_summaries

| Recipient state | Next step |
|---|---|
| No reply yet | Offer to clarify how the evidence extractions map to specific ACMG criteria, if that would help the curation team. |
| Replied with interest | Ask which variants they want to start with and offer to discuss the evidence code mapping. |

## After all three products delivered

| Recipient state | Next step |
|---|---|
| No reply yet | Pick the one product most relevant to their current curation cycle (from intake record or prior thread) and reference a specific finding from that one only. Don't try to reference all three. |
| Replied positively | Propose a 20-minute call to discuss the findings and agree on what to do next. |

## Additional genes pending

If the intake record or prior thread shows additional genes the recipient asked about (e.g., Amanda flagged OTC for all three products), always offer to queue the next gene by name. This is the highest-value next step when it applies — it extends the relationship without requiring a call.

## When no report has been delivered yet

Do not use this skill. Use `dgai-outreach` if the relationship is still in the offer stage.
